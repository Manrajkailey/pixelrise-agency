Who We Are

A Team Obsessed With Your Growth

Founded in 2019, PixelRise Digital started as a two-person SEO consultancy in Ludhiana. Today, we're a 25-strong team of digital strategists, SEO specialists, paid media experts, content creators, and web developers — all united by a single obsession: generating measurable results for our clients.

We've helped over 300 brands across India, the UK, and the US grow their organic traffic, reduce their cost-per-lead, and turn their websites into their most powerful sales channels. Our approach is simple: understand your business deeply, craft a strategy built around your goals, execute with precision, and refine relentlessly.

We're not the biggest agency. We're the most invested one. Every client gets a dedicated account manager, transparent monthly reporting, and a team that treats your marketing budget like it's our own money.
